Frequently Asked Questions

How do I rent a tool?

Simply browse our available tools, select the one you need, fill out the rental form with your details and preferred dates, and submit your request. We'll confirm your booking within 24 hours.

What if I need to extend my rental period?

You can extend your rental period by contacting us before your return date. Additional charges will apply based on our daily rates.

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes, we offer delivery services within a 20-mile radius for an additional fee. Contact us to arrange delivery for your rental.

What happens if a tool gets damaged?

Normal wear and tear is expected. However, any damage beyond normal use will result in repair charges. We recommend inspecting tools upon pickup and return.
